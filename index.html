About Litends

A small studio with a focused mission

Litends was founded in Bremen, Germany with a simple idea: make AI and modern web development accessible to businesses that don't have big tech budgets. We're a young startup, and we're honest about that.

What we bring is deep technical knowledge, genuine care for our clients, and the agility to move fast. We don't promise to be the biggest agency in the room — we aim to be the most attentive one.

We believe in transparent communication, fair pricing, and delivering work we're proud of. Every project is a partnership, and we measure our success by yours.

What we value

  • Honesty over hype

    We'll tell you if AI isn't the right fit for your problem.

  • Quality craftsmanship

    Clean code, thoughtful design, tested thoroughly.

  • Transparent pricing

    No hidden fees. You know the scope and cost before we start.

  • Long-term thinking

    We build solutions that scale, not quick fixes that break.
